Pani-puri

Description

Pani puri, also known as golgappa, puchka, or fuchka, is a beloved Indian street food originating from the Indian subcontinent. It consists of a crispy, hollow puri (bread) filled with a mixture of flavored water (pani), tamarind chutney, chili powder, and various fillings such as potatoes, onions, chickpeas, and sprouts.

Ingredients

  • Puri
  • Filling
  • The spices and tangy Pani

Steps to make Pani Puri

  1. Assemble the puri on the plate.
  2. Then fill 2 or 3 tablespoon of boondi in the puri.
  3. The stir the pani nicely.
  4. Pour pani in the puri. Fill it till the top.
  5. Then enjoy !
