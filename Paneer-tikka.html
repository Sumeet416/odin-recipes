Paneer-tikka

Description

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made with marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cubes, grilled to perfection. The dish typically involves a spiced yogurt-based marinade, which helps to tenderize the paneer and infuse it with flavors.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Yogurt
  • Spices
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Oil or Butter
  • Green Chutney

Steps to make Paneer Tikka

  1. Marinate the paneer
  2. Pan-Fry the paneer
  3. Prepare the sauce
  4. Assemble and Serve
